Meet Our Poets

Maya Angelou

American poet, dancer, singer, and activist. Maya Angelou was a world-famous author and is an honorary member at poelette as many of us are inspired by her work.

Ruthie Boat

Ruthie Boat is a Ghanaian poet, singer-songwriter, educator and an inspiring creator in a wealth of fields. She is the author of Paediatric Poetry series, a poetry book series for children.

Amanda Gorman

Amanda Gorman is an American poet and activist.
Her work focuses on issues of oppression, feminism, race and marginalization, as well as the African diaspora. She published the poetry book in 2015.

Uzodinma Iweala

Uzodinma Iweala is a Nigerian-American
author and novelist. His debut novel, Beasts of No Nation, which became a movie and earned him much recognition was a formation of his thesis work in creative writing at Harvard.
